In finding solutions I don’t limit myself to one discipline within design, I take a multidisciplinary approach. The process starts by looking at the surrounding factors and environment. Understanding the bigger picture. With this research led approach, I strive towards simplicity and clarity in my designs.

In 2014 I started studying Industrial Product Design at HAN University of Applied Sciences. Since then I have interned and worked at multiple design agencies as industrial designer.

In 2019 I completed Co-Design Studio, a full-time minor in human centered design, working for real clients with coaching by experienced design professionals.
